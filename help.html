How To Play



1. Press the rocket on the home page to launch the game
2. When the game loads you are asked if you want to help out, choose yes or no, as whoen in the example below
3. Extra coins are givin if you get questions correct.
4. Your choices change the game and ends the game very differently so you choose.
5. Your total at the end also determines your score
6. Create a username if you wish or use default of Rocket Fox
