The land of the animals are under threat, what is happening to the land and why?. To find the answers Rocket Fox has set out on a mission to help in an attempt to save the land and the animals who reside. Play as Rocket Fox, as he uses his rocket car to travel fast and his animal friends help him on his journey, complete puzzles and help animal friends out, however it ends is down to the choices you make through the game. Can you save the land.
